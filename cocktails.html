Hemmingway

Instructions and ingredients

Pour all ingredients into a champagne flute and stir.

  • Four cl champagne
  • Two cl vodka
  • One cl absinthe

Mojito

Instructions and ingredients

Pour all ingredients into a cocktail shaker and shake. Serve over ice.

  • Three cl white rum
  • Six cl lime juice
  • Two spoons of sugar

Daiquiri

Instructions and ingredients

Put all ingredients into a blender and serve.

  • One banana
  • Four cl cream
  • Two cl rum

Martini

Instructions and ingredients

Mix in cocktail shaker and serve in Martini glass.

  • Four cl fortified wine
  • Six cl lime juice
  • Two cl vodka
